Virtual Reality has been something new added to video games because
it allows players to have a whole different aspect of the game. You are
literally driven into the game and your own movements are what is
happening on the screen. Some of the games that have been released
were, Batman: Arkham VR, Creed: Rise To Glory, Borderlands, Golf,
Five Nights at Freddy's VR. These games are all available through
the PlayStation store and all you have to do is buy a VR head
set and purchase a game to start your experience. It is way
different from a 3rd point of view and pressing buttons on
controller and you commanding the character what to do.
VR is being implemented in such various ways that it
has exceed people's thoughts about changes that
can happen. Apart from using VR in video games, it
is also being used other ways to help people fight
their anxities, traumas, and fears.
