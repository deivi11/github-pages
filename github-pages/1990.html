In 1989, Gameboy was a portable gaming device that became a success.
Over the course of time Gameboy has sold over 150 million systems worldwide.
In 1990, SNES 'Super Nintendo Entertainment System', released its 16-bit tech-
nology processor which meant more entertaining games that helped sell 49 million
systems worldwide. Games such as 'Street Fighter', 'Mortal Kombat', and etc.
Based on these fighting games the issue of promoting violence aroused and it
wasn't good for the kids playing these games so the 'Videogame Rating Council'
made the industry Entertainment Software Rating Board, to keep a restriction on
games with certain profiles to appropriate age wise. In 1996, the Nintendo 64 was
released and it was also a major hit because of the amazing graphics and gameplay.
