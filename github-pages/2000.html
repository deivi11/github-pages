This era is well known to those born in the millennial because we basically
grew up with video games surrounding us. Sony dominated the video game
market by introducing good quality games plus the integration of the Play
Station 2, which allowed gamers to play video games from the original Play
Station. This allowed gamers not throw away their money from the original
PlayStation games and also being able to use DVD's in the PS2. In 2001,
the Nintendo released a console named, GameCube, which would be the
first to use optimize disks and would be out there in the market as an
upgrade to the Nintendo 64. Nintendo has released so many consoles from
home consoles to portable devices such as the Nintendo DS, WII, and other
upgraded versions. In 2003, Microsoft introduced the Xbox 360 which would
become a popular console making a competition for other gaming consoles.
Up to this day there is still debates and arguments over the Xbox and the
PlayStation to see which one is better and its almost a 50/50 share.
