After a decade of trying to create computer games, programmers made
these games work. The first computer game based was called 'SpaceWar'.
SpaceWar was invented by MIT student Steve Russel. The game became
so popular it spread across the country. By 1965, the first football game
was created by another student. Creating games was such an innovation
at that time that in 1966 Ralph Baer decided that he wanted to play video
games through a television screen. He created, "Brown Box" prototype.
