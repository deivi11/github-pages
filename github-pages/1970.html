The rise of arcade games came upon the existince of computer game,
'SpaceWar'. Since it was a hit when it was on PC, Nolan Bushnell and
Ted Dabney, converted the PC game into the first arcade game. The
first home video game system was called, 'Atari' in 1972. Atari's "Pong",
was the first arcade video game. Later that year in 1975, a home version
of 'Pong' was realesed for computers which was also a success. In 1977,
Atari redesigned the home computer by adding a joystick. A noteable
change in the gaming industry came when the first third-party game
developer, Activision, developed software without making consoles.
Nintendo's creation of Donkey Kong, introduced the world to the famous
character of Mario.
